步驟 1/3 先處理來源缺口 / Resolve source gaps first

沒有來源的項目不可視為完成審核。 / Items without sources are not review-ready.

步驟 2/3 檢查到期審核 / Review due items

步驟 3/3 開啟項目並記錄決策 / Open the item and record a decision

每次核准或退回都會留下審核紀錄。 / Every approval or requested change is recorded.
